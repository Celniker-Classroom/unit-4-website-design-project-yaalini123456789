Basic Tips for Table Tennis Page!

Rules of table tennis, equipment, and rating information. Additionally, information on various styles of playing.

I am a professional table tennis player, so here some valuable and reliable tips to improve.

  • Learn a basic Forehand and Backhand
  • Learn a Push which is more defensive
  • Learn a serve for all spins
  • Learn an opening for attacking and getting rallies in
  • Work on Footwork drills and Body turn for more power
  • I recommend going to tournaments to gain expereince. Check the tournament page for more response.
  • I recommend training at a club to get personalized experience.
  • Try out different styles to figure out which one you like best.
  • I recommend training against or with various styles for more experience.
