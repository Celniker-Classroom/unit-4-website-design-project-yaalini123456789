Rules of table tennis, equipment, and rating information. Additionally, information on various styles of playing.

Rules:

  1. First to 11
  2. Duce at 10-10 and every tied score after that
  3. Win by 2 points
  4. Usually Best of 5 - First to win 3 games
  5. Need a Racket, Table and shoes for professional
  6. Attacking style (How I play) Short Pips/Regular rubber
  7. Defending style - Choppers/long pips
