What do you get from tournaments?

Tournaments can provide a lot of experience against other styled players to see where your real level is to know what to improve on.

Tournaments also have prizes for various amounts of money, trophies or others.

Tournaments have rated events and age events.

But, what is rating?

Table tennis ratings measure player skill levels using a numerical system. Ratings typically range from beginners (under 1000 rating) to elite pros (2500+), with 2000+ representing advanced, semi-pro, or competitive club players. Ratings are based on Elo-style formulas, increasing with every tournament win. With earning up to 50 points for one game or even losing the same amount.
